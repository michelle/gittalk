yummy apple pie

4 apples, peeled
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup splenda
1/8 cup brown splenda 
1/8 cup flour
1 tsp cinnammon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
3 cups butter
1 egg yolk
1 tsp milk



mix everything except the egg yolk and milk together until it looks like apple pie filling. place inside apple pie pastry.

cover with top crust. beat yolk and milk and spread over top crust.

bake at 425 degrees for 15 mins.
