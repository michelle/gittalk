BACON EXPLOSION

10 pounds thick cut bacon
50 pounds Italian sausage
25 jars of your favorite barbeque sauce
25 jars of your favorite barbeque rub





CREATE 5X5 BACON WEAVES. MAKE EM TIGHT. ADD BBQ RUB. RUB IT ALLLL OVER.

NOW LAYER EACH WEAVE WITH 2 LBS OF ITALIAN SAUSAGE.

THEN PUT MORE BACON ON TOP.

COOL. NOW DRIZZLE ON THAT BBQ SAUCE. NO, WAIT, DUMP ON THAT BBQ SAUCE. AND ROLL THAT BABY UP.

ENJOY YOUR CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASE OF AWESOMENESS.
